Fitt's Law

Sololearn www.sololearn.com

Fitt's Law states the the longer the distance and the smaller the target's size, the longer it takes. On this site we see large enough buttons that the user can easily and quickly press.

PARC: Proximity

Neuralink www.neuralink.com

Proximity helps to illustrate relationships among objects through the distance between them. On this site we see that title, paragraph and two buttons are related. But these objects together are a separate entity relative to the next title.

PARC: Contrast

Blue Origin www.blueorigin.com

Contrast compels the observer to notice important detail within the work through size, color, or cintent contrast. On this site we see the color contrast between background and text color. Through this we concentrate on the text and options.
