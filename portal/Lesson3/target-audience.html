Target Audience

Who: Everyone who needs to check the weather for different situations.

Age: 6+

Motivation: To be prepared for the weater at the right time and place.

Personas

Dmitriy Kudasov

Demographics: 50 years old, works in IT, married, has 5 children

Motivations for using: Planning a family activities. Because some children live in another cities, he needs to know what the weather is there to plan a trip to his children.

Technology: Uses an iPhone, iPad and MacBook Pro.

Mariya Petuhova

Demographics: 21 years old, works in Auditing Company, single adult

Motivations for using: She loves to travel and for planning her trip she needs to know about weather in a place which she wants to visit.

Technology: Uses an iPhone and iPad

Scenarios

1. What's the weather going to be this weekend at the ski base? Will there be enough snow?

2. What clothes should I take on a trip to England?

3. It will be rain today? Maybe I should take an umbrella?

4. Is it warm enough today to go to the sea?

5. Last year winter was very snowy and cold, I wonder what it will be this year?
